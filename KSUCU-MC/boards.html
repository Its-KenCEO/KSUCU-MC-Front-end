KSUCU-MC BOARDS

Kisii University Christian Union Main Campus so far has 3 registered boards i.e ICT Board, Media Board, Edditorial Board, and Communication Board.

ICT BOARD

ICT Board is a Board registered under Kisii University Christian Union Main Campus.
It is entitled with the following

  • It is entitled with the following
  • Projection during the services and other KSUCU-MC acctivities
  • Management of KSUCU-MC website
  • Management of KSUCU-MC Facebook page
  • Management of KSUCU-MC Database

MEDIA BOARD

A registered board of Kisii University its mandated to do the following

  • Coverage of KSUCU-MC services and other KSUCU-MC acctivities
  • Management of KSUCU-MC YOUTUBE Channel
  • Capturing of Photos during kSUCU-MC activities
  • Edditing of Videos

EDITORIAL BOARD

A registered board of Kisii University its mandated to do the following

  • Publication of KSUCU-MC Magazines
  • Publication of KSUCU-MC pamphlets

COMMUNICATION BOARD

Communication Board is a Board registered under Kisii University Christian Union Main Campus. It is entitled with the following

  • All KSUCU-MC communications are made through this board
  • Aproving of KSUCU-MC posters ad sharing
